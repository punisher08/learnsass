SEO-Search Engine Optimization

Moving into the top three positions on Google is the goal for most practices investing in digital marketing because it offers the highest possibility of CTR (Click Through Rate) for relevant keywords and therefore the highest ROI (Return on Investment). The higher on Google your practice ranks for the top searched term(s) the higher the practices’ revenues and overall net worth.

Knowledge in many specific areas of SEO expertise is required to rank your practice on Google for your top searched for keywords and are imperative to a successful SEO campaign.

The most important factors that are:

  • Custom Specialized Content Creation.

  • Page Load Speed (Esp. Mobile Page Speed).
  • Mobile Friendliness.
  • Keyword Research.
  • Technical SEO.
  • Local SEO.
  • User Experience
  • Social Media Marketing
